Ralph Billion values, Your dream our commitment

Ralph Billion Values Limited (RC 1862163) is a fast-growing Indigenous Real Estate and Construction Company in Nigeria with interest in Asset valuation, Property Acquisition, Development, Sales and Management. With a team of experienced Engineers, Surveyors and Managers, we provide professional Real Estate Service to both Local and International Clients..

Ralph Billion Values is supported by an integrated quality management system that underpins every aspect of its operations. Quality management is the cornerstone of RBV operational culture and business approach. We have over-arching measures to consistently monitor performance and aim to empower employees to hive its corporate values.

Values: Wherever in the country it operates, Ralph Billion Values strives to live by its core values of people, excellence, transformation, customer-focus, innovation, integrity and Performance.

Diversification: We are a RealEstate and Construction Services company with diversification in supply and installation of aluminium and stainless steel railings, aluminium Windows, glasswork;glass balcony, glass shade, foldable glass,rolling glass doors, all types of glass doors, carports and overhead tanks and general construction.

Real Estate Services: Searching for the right property for you is our main responsibility and we take it seriously. We will work with you to understand your needs, requests, and personal lifestyle and diversify our strategy to capture the ideal solution. Our communication is comprehensive and transparent, we will cooperate with you to make the right selection and ensure that you are satisfied with the outcome. Ralph Billion Values Limited leads the Real Estate market in Nigeria - based on a solid network that takes care of the details - able to guarantee you the most professional property management service. Construction Management Ralph Billion Values offer professional construction management services that match the size of any given project. Innovative development, avant-garde architectural designs and eco-friendly materials have been integrated in our construction process giving a new style into buildings. We provide construction services that enable our clients to achieve the best value and security on their investment, while constructing their dream property in Nigeria.
